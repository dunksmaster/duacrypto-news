**Is Bitcoin legal in Albania?** We hear this at every meetup — from Tirana, Prishtina, and the **diaspora** in Rome and Munich.

This English guide pairs with our Albanian post [A është Bitcoin i ligjshëm në Shqipëri?](/posts/a-eshte-bitcoin-i-ligjshem-ne-shqiperi/). It is **education**, not legal or tax advice.

## Short answer

Bitcoin is widely used by individuals via **international exchanges**; it is **not** the official currency of Albania and does not have the same consumer protections as a bank deposit. That gap is why we emphasize **reputable on-ramps** and **self-custody** after learning.

## What people actually do

1. Register on exchanges such as [Bitget](/go/bitget) or [CEX.io](/go/cex).
2. Complete **KYC** with real ID.
3. Buy spot BTC in **EUR/USD**.
4. Withdraw to a [hardware wallet](/en/posts/tangem-hardware-wallet-albania/) for savings.

## Banks and cards

Some Albanian cards work; some block crypto MCC codes. A block is not proof Bitcoin is “illegal” — it is often bank risk policy. Do **not** respond by sending money to Telegram “traders”.
