## Before you start

- **Volatility** — only invest what you can afford to lose.
- **Scams** — never share seed phrases; avoid Telegram OTC brokers (see our Albanian scam guide, EN version coming).
- **Tax** — diaspora readers may owe tax where they live; this is education, not tax advice.

## Step 2 — Security basics

1. Unique email + strong password.
2. **2FA** on day one.
3. Bookmark official URLs — no DMs from "support".

Detailed registration: [Bitget guide](/en/posts/bitcoin-self-custody-basics/) (security overlap) — we are publishing a dedicated EN Bitget walkthrough next.

## Step 3 — Buy spot BTC (not leverage)

Deposit **€20–50** first. Buy **BTC spot**. Confirm balance. You still do **not** have self-custody until withdrawal.

## Step 4 — Self-custody for savings

When ready, withdraw to a [hardware wallet](/go/tangem):

- Generate receive address in wallet app.
- Withdraw test amount.
- Then move the rest.
