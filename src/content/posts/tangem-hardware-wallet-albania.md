## Why leave coins on the exchange?

Exchanges can freeze withdrawals, get hacked, or fail compliance checks. For **long-term savings**, move BTC to a wallet only you control.

## Why Tangem?

- **Card form factor** — NFC tap with phone app.
- No monthly subscription for basic self-custody.
- Popular with readers who want simpler UX than full seed-phrase laptops.

Order only via [official Tangem link](/go/tangem) — avoid second-hand cards or Telegram “deals”.

## Setup checklist

1. Initialize the card **yourself** — never use a pre-seeded device.
2. Write backup/recovery info **offline** — metal or paper in a safe place.
3. Generate a receive address in the app.
4. From the exchange, withdraw **10–20 EUR equivalent** as a test.
5. After confirmation, withdraw the rest in tranches if the amount is large.

## Diaspora angle
