The Balkans Crypto Tech Conference returned to Tirana on 15–16 June 2025, bringing together DeFi builders, Web3 founders, and Bitcoin educators from across the region.

## Conference highlights

Expo booths from major exchanges, networking sessions, and community meetups filled two days of programming. DuaCrypto hosted side events and connected local students with industry mentors.

## Community impact

For Albania's crypto community, Balkans Crypto 2025 validated years of grassroots education work. From our first Tirana meetups in 2020 to a regional conference stage — the roadmap is working.
