DuaCrypto is Albania's longest-running Bitcoin and crypto community. Joining is free — here's how to get started.

## 1. Join Telegram

Our primary hub is [t.me/dua_crypto](https://t.me/dua_crypto) with 10,000+ members. Introduce yourself, ask questions, and follow event announcements.

## 2. Read free guides

Long-form education lives at [duacrypto.org](https://duacrypto.org/) — Bitcoin basics, wallet setup, and Balkans-specific context.

## 3. Attend a meetup

We host monthly events in Tirana — Bitcoin Pizza Day, Balkans Crypto side sessions, and beginner workshops. Check our [events page](https://duacrypto.com/events.html).

## 4. Go deeper

Corporate teams: explore [DAL membership](https://duacrypto.com/bitcoin-for-corporations.html). Researchers: subscribe to our [newsletter](https://duacrypto.com/newsletter.html).
