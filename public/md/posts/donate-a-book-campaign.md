Every ~150,000 sats (~$110 USD) sends one quality Bitcoin book to a student in the Balkans. 100% of Lightning donations fund books — no admin cut.

## How it works

You donate sats via Lightning → we pool funds → our publishing partner Konsensus Network sources books from [bitcoinbook.shop](https://bitcoinbook.shop) → students receive them.

## Featured title

We start with *Un scénario optimiste pour Bitcoin* by Vijay Boyapati — a French edition covering Bitcoin's bullish case, self-custody, and monetary history.

## Goal: 1,000 books

We've funded 24 books so far toward a 1,000-book target. Scan the QR on our [donation page](https://duacrypto.com/donation.html) with any Lightning wallet.
