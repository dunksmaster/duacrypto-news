Every year on May 22, Bitcoiners worldwide celebrate the first real-world Bitcoin purchase — two pizzas for 10,000 BTC in 2010. DuaCrypto marked the occasion with a community meetup at The Taproom in Tirana.

## Why Pizza Day matters

Pizza Day isn't about the price of those coins today — it's about proving Bitcoin works as money. For newcomers in Albania, it's the perfect entry point to understand why we build self-custody skills and long-term thinking.
